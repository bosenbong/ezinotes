# EziNotes - Founding Document

## Purpose
EziNotes is an AI-powered tool for support workers (NDIS and similar) that converts voice notes into structured, professional, compliant session notes.

## Problem
Support workers spend unpaid time writing notes. It is slow, repetitive, and often poor quality.

## Solution
Users speak naturally → AI transcribes → AI formats into structured, NDIS-style notes → export or copy.

## Target Users
- Support workers
- Disability support providers
- Sole traders and agencies

## Founder Info
- Based in Australia (WA)
- Runs service business (Coastal Garden & Maintenance)
- Real-world experience as support worker
- Not a developer
- Wants to build fast, validate early, then scale

## Constraints
- Must eventually be privacy-first
- Must support Australian hosting (future AWS Sydney)
- Must be simple to use on mobile
- MVP should be fast and cheap to build

## First Task - 10 Questions for Founder

1. **Who is your ideal first customer?** (specific role, organisation type, location)
2. **What's your budget for the MVP?** (time and money)
3. **How do you plan to get users initially?** (networks, social media, cold outreach)
4. **What does "compliant" mean exactly?** (which NDIS/state guidelines must be met)
5. **What's the core voice input method?** (app recording, WhatsApp voice note, phone call)
6. **Do you need offline capability?** (support workers in remote areas)
7. **What's the single most important output format?** (PDF, Word, plain text, app display)
8. **Do you need multi-user/team features now?** (or single user fine for MVP)
9. **What's your timeline for a usable beta?** (weeks, months)
10. **Are you okay with overseas AI processing now, or must it be AU-hosted from day one?**

---

## Founder Answers

1. **Ideal First Customer:** Independent support workers (sole traders) in Australia using platforms like Mable or working privately. Overwhelmed with admin.

2. **MVP Budget:** Time: 1–2 hours/day. Money: Under $200 to start.

3. **Initial User Acquisition:** Personal network from support work, local contacts, direct outreach (text/FB groups), TikTok documentation.

4. **Compliant Means:** Clear, factual, objective notes. Professional tone. NDIS-style reporting. No medical claims. Structured, consistent format.

5. **Core Voice Input:** Upload voice recording OR record directly in browser. Keep it simple.

6. **Offline Capability:** Not required for MVP.

7. **Output Format:** Plain text first (copy/paste). PDF export later.

8. **Multi-User:** No — single user only for MVP.

9. **Timeline:** Usable beta in 7–14 days.

10. **AI Processing:** Overseas OK for MVP. Long-term goal: Australian-hosted.
